Designing Rewarding Power-ups

Why shields, slow-mo and multipliers keep players returning.

Published 2025-08-22

Why shields, slow-mo and multipliers keep players returning.

Give second chances

A temporary shield lets players experiment without losing progress, raising engagement and skill.

Create drama with slow-time

Brief slow-motion during spikes helps players regain control and feel powerful.

Reward streaks

Score multipliers tied to consecutive successes produce a satisfying rhythm; decay them gently, don’t reset harshly.

Telegraph spawns

Subtle cues (glow, sound) make power-ups feel fair, not random.
